## Game content

The underlying names, ids, and stats describe Age of Empires III: Definitive
Edition, which is © Microsoft. Only factual text and numeric ids are stored in
this repository. No copyrighted image/audio asset is bundled — icon paths are
references to be satisfied by a user-local game-file extraction.
